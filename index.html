About Me

I work as a Junior Research Fellow with Prof. Anirban Dasgupta at the Indian Institute of Technology Gandhinagar, India. My research interest broadly lies in the area of machine learning.

I received the Director’s Silver Medal and my Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science & Engineering in 2020 from IIT Gandhinagar. Previously, I had interned at Caltech (with Dr. Ashish Mahabal in 2019), Microsoft Research India (with Dr. Harsha Vardhan Simhadri in 2019), and IISc (with Prof. Chandra Sekhar Seelamantula in 2017).
